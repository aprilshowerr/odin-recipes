Green Chili Anchovies Fried Rice

Description

If you are bored with processed chicken and meat, you can make fried rice with a mixture of anchovies and green chilies. This fried rice besides have delicious and more savory taste, the color is also very beautiful.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Anchovies
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 green chilies
  • 5 Bird's eye chilies
  • Broccoli (optionally replace existing vegetables: mustard greens, cabbage etc.)
  • 5 shallots
  • 3 garlic
  • pepper
  • Shrimp paste (tastier and more aromatic)

Steps

  1. Fry anchovies, set aside
  2. Fried 1 egg, set aside
  3. Chilies, shallots, garlic, pepper, shrimp paste, salt (grind coarsely)
  4. Saute the spices in oil, when they smell good, add 1 egg, scramble it
  5. Add broccoli (pre-boiled)/other vegetables according to taste
  6. Add white rice, stir until well mixed
  7. Taste test, can add salt/flavoring to taste
  8. Serve on a plate, add a fried egg on top
