Homemade Chicken Meatball

Description

Chicken meatballs are meatballs with the main raw material of chicken meat with the addition of spices as meatballs in general. Come on, try this fresh, delicious and nutritious chicken meatball soup recipe.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken Meatballs:

    • 150g chicken breast
    • 35gr chopped carrot
    • 1 egg white
    • 3 tbsp tapioca flour
    • 1 garlic (grind)
    • Salt, stock powder, pepper (to taste)
    • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
    • 1/2 tsp sesame oil

  2. Soup + Vegetable:

    • 1 stalks of green onions
    • Bok choy/green mustard (to taste)
    • Salt, stock powder, pepper powder (to taste)
    • 500ml water (to taste)
    • A little soy sauce & sesame oil

Steps

  1. Put the chicken breast that has been mashed/chopped into the container.
  2. Add all other ingredients and stir until evenly smooth
  3. Chop green mustard and green onion
  4. Heat the air until it boils
  5. Round up the meatball mixture with a spoon, then add to the pot
  6. Wait until the meatballs float
  7. Add salt, stock powder, ground pepper, soy sauce and sesame oil
  8. Taste test
  9. Add vegetables, then serve
