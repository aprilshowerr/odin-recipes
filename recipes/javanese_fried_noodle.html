Javanese Fried Noodle

Description

Who doesn't love fried noodles? One variant of fried noodles that is delicious to enjoy is Javanese fried noodles. The difference between Javanese fried noodles and other fried noodles is the use of candlenut and shrimp in the ground spices.

Ingredients

  • 100gr chicken
  • 1 pack of dried noodles
  • 1 bunch of mustard greens
  • Cabbage/cobbage to taste
  • 3 stalks of green onions
  • 1 tomato
  • Sausages / meatballs (optional)
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 4 tbsp sweet soy sauce / to taste
  • 1/2 tsp stock powder
  • Fried shallots

Ground spices

  • 1/2 tsp pepper seeds
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 5 cloves of shallot
  • 3 toasted candlenuts
  • 5 medium sized shrimp with skin on (key to good seasoning)

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken until cooked (shredded), use the remaining cooking water to boil the noodles (drain)
  2. Grind all the ground spices
  3. Chop vegetables and sausages / meatballs (optional)
  4. Saute all the ground spices with oil until fragrant and browned, set aside.
  5. Add 1 egg, then scramble gently
  6. Add shredded chicken, meatballs / sausages and add a little water
  7. Add salt, coconut sugar, broth powder stock powder
  8. Add boiled noodles, stir until the spices and noodles are evenly combined
  9. Add soy sauce (according to taste)
  10. Add chopped vegetables
  11. Stir and taste test according to taste, serve with fried shallot flakes
